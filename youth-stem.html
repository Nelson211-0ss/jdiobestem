Youth STEM School Program

Empowering youth to innovate, research, and excel through hands-on STEM training and mentorship.

Program Overview

Our Youth STEM school program is focused on inspiring and empowering youth to engage and innovate in STEM. Specifically, our program provides students with the training, resources, and mentorship required to build competence and skills to pursue STEM careers, conduct independent research projects, innovations and foster a deep interest in STEM fields.

Our innovative approach integrates practical hands-on skilling in various STEM fields such as ICT, mobile Apps development, software development, robotics, UAV technology aerospace and coding with soft skills training such as communication, leadership, financial literacy and analytical thinking. This comprehensive approach ensures students develop both technical expertise and essential professional skills.
