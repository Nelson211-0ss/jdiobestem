About the institute

The Aerospace Institute offers focused programs, research opportunities, and industry partnerships for students passionate about aerospace engineering and flight systems. We emphasize rigorous fundamentals, hands-on build-test cycles, and professional skills that transfer to university and the workforce.

Whether you are prototyping unmanned systems, studying materials and structures, or learning how aircraft are certified and operated safely, the institute connects classroom theory to real engineering practice.
