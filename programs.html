Program Overview

We focus on the barriers that most often stop students from moving forward.

Our programs are designed to support students at multiple points in their journey. Some students need financial assistance. Others need exposure, mentorship, career guidance, or a chance to apply what they are learning through real projects.

Instead of treating these needs separately, we connect scholarships, training, mentorship, and innovation support into a pathway that helps students build both opportunity and confidence.

Core Programs

Four connected areas of support

Each program is designed to be practical, measurable, and directly connected to student success.

Removing financial barriers

Scholarships Program

We provide financial support to students pursuing STEM education, helping reduce the cost barriers that prevent talented learners from continuing their academic journey.

Who it serves

Students from underserved communities in Uganda who are pursuing or preparing to pursue STEM education.

Learn about scholarships

What it includes

  • Tuition support
  • Learning materials
  • Academic related expenses
  • Travel or access needs when applicable

Expected outcome

Students are better able to enroll, remain in school, and continue progressing toward STEM degrees and careers.
