Secondary School Research

Labs, mentors, and partnerships that turn curiosity into UAV, software, robotics, and aerospace prototypes.

Program overview

The research, innovation, and development program nurtures secondary students building real projects in STEM—with emphasis on UAVs, software, robotics, and aerospace. We work toward modern facilities, global collaborations, and funding pathways so ideas become tested systems and publications.
