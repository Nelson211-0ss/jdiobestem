Secondary School Research Program

Fostering research, innovation, and product development in STEM for the next generation.

Program Overview

The research, innovation and development program aims to foster research and product development in STEM fields especially UAVs, software, robotics and aerospace by providing state of the art research facilities, nurturing global collaborations and mobilizing investment funding to support research and innovations.

Hands-on experience in school and partner labs.

Learn to collect, analyze, and present scientific data.

Mentored Projects

Work with STEM mentors on real-world research topics.
