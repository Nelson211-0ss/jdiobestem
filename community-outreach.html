Community STEM Outreach

Practical ICT and innovation skills for out-of-school youth and refugees—so everyone can participate in the digital economy.

Program overview

This program equips out-of-school youth and refugees in Uganda with hands-on ICT and computer skills for employment and entrepreneurship. It bridges gaps in mobile apps, software, robotics, and computer repair—while sparking innovations in agriculture, health, finance, education, and business through regional and national challenges and science fairs.

By strengthening technical fluency and creative problem solving, participants can build livelihoods and contribute more fully to their communities.
