Scholarship Program

Empowering students to achieve their dreams in STEM through financial support and opportunity.

Program Overview

Our scholarship program is designed to support high-achieving and financially deserving young people to pursue advanced education in STEM fields. Through our scholarship program we intend to address financial constraints to STEM education by providing tuition, encourage academic excellence, and promote diversity within STEM disciplines in Uganda.

Scholarship Benefits

Financial Aid

Tuition support for outstanding STEM students.

Mentorship

Guidance from STEM professionals and alumni.

Recognition

Opportunities to present research and achievements.
