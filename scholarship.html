Scholarship Program

Financial support and recognition for high-achieving students so cost never blocks a STEM future.

Who we support

High-achieving and financially deserving young people in Uganda preparing for advanced STEM study.

What we fund

Tuition support, learning materials, and travel where needed—aligned with STEM pathways.

Why it matters

Diversity and excellence in STEM start with removing financial barriers to education.

Program overview

Our scholarship program supports high-achieving and deserving young people to pursue advanced education in STEM. We aim to ease financial constraints, encourage academic excellence, and strengthen diversity within STEM disciplines in Uganda—through tuition assistance, materials, and connections to mentorship.

Scholarship benefits

A holistic wraparound so recipients can focus on learning and growth.

Financial aid

Tuition and essential costs for outstanding STEM students.

Mentorship

Guidance from STEM professionals and alumni networks.

Recognition

Platforms to showcase research, projects, and achievements.
